Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

This Privacy Policy outlines the manner in which we collect, use, and safeguard user information in connection with our digital services and offerings.

1. Information We Collect

We are committed to minimizing the collection of personal data. The only information we collect from users is:

  • Username
  • Email address

We do not collect or store passwords, payment information, or any other personally sensitive data.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

The information collected is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Order processing and delivery of digital goods
  • Customer support and purchase-related communication
  • Account identification and product access management

We do not share, sell, or distribute user data to third parties under any circumstances.

3. Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable measures to protect the limited data we collect. All information is stored in secure environments and managed with appropriate access controls.

4. Data Retention

User information is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes stated in this policy, or as required by applicable law.

5. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in legal requirements or operational practices. Users will be notified of any material updates, and continued use of our services after such changes constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.

6. Contact Information

For any inquiries or concerns related to this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us through our support channels or Discord server.
